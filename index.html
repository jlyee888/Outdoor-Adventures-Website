About this site...

Welcome to my outdoor adventures website! I made this website because I wanted to create a web tool to help plan hikes and other outdoor adventures in my home county and its surrounding area. Under each regional area tab, one can find hike/backpacking planning resources to make their next trip a success! In the future, I will implement more pages detailing hiking information for different national forests and wilderness areas. I will also try to turn part of this website into a blog so you can read about my outdoor adventures.

As I continue to get better at website making, newer features will streamline your hike-planning process so stay tuned! :)

A little about me...

I'm a SoCal native that loves being in the outdoors. As a Boy Scout, I backpacked 5 days in the Sangre de Cristo Mountains in New Mexico's Philmont Scout Ranch. Since then, I've enjoyed the solitude, challenge, and mental comfort of being in the great outdoors.
